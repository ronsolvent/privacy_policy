Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

AlterPath ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

AlterPath collects the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personality Data: Profile data generated from your story choices and interactions within the app.
  • Progress & Achievements: Data related to your story progress, unlocked archetypes, and achievements.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app to help us improve performance and user experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device details for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized story recommendations based on your personality profile.
  • Track your progress and maintain your unique archetype history.
  • Improve app functionality, fix bugs, and optimize user experience.
  • Send you notifications regarding daily rewards or friend activities (if you have enabled them).

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.
  • In the cloud (via Google Firebase) to enable backup, synchronization across devices, and social features.

All data transmission between the app and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS).

Third-Party Services

AlterPath integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: For analytics, authentication, and cloud storage.
  • Google AdMob: To display rewarded advertisements (if applicable).
  • Google Play Services: For core Android functionality.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of personalized analytics or advertising.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

AlterPath is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it immediately.
